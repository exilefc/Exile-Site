Exile FC (Mistguard of Nabudis)

Formed 04/04/2014 (Tonberry)

We are Mistguard, the Guardian Force of Eorzea! We welcome all players new and old, with a focus on building a helpful community. Join us today!

About

We're an English-speaking Free Company from the Tonberry Server! We've been around a long time and are made up of all sorts of players: crafters and raiders, new players and veterans, and everything in-between.

This site is intended as a resource for members and non-members alike. If you have questions or feedback come chat with us on our Discord (link below).

Discord Channel

Exile FC has a Discord channel! The majority of our news and discussion will take place there. The channel is open to non-members as well! Press connect below to join.
